EL POLLO LOCO

Join Pepe on a wild desert adventure to defeat the giant chicken and save the day!

Key Legend for Pepe's moves

  • walk left: Left Arrow Key
    Pepe moves left.
  • walk right: Right Arrow Key
    Pepe moves right.
  • jump: Spacebar
    Pepe jumps over obstacles or enemies.
  • throw bottle: Key D
    Pepe throws bottle.

The Story of Pepe

Pepe, the brave Mexican, embarks on an exciting adventure through the hot desert. Along his journey, he encounters dangerous enemies in the form of wild chickens blocking his way.

However, Pepe can defeat these chickens with throwing bottles he collects from the ground. Besides the chickens, there are also valuable coins to be found in the desert, which Pepe can collect to regain health.

At the end of his journey, a great final boss - a giant chicken - awaits to be defeated in order to win the game.

Game Rules

Pepe can move left and right using the arrow keys and jump using the spacebar. Collect bottles and coins scattered throughout the desert.

- Health Bar: Shows Pepe's remaining health.
- Bottle Bar: Shows the number of collected bottles.
- Coin Bar: Shows the number of collected coins.

Pepe can defeat chickens by jumping on them or throwing collected bottles. The final boss, a giant chicken, can be defeated by hitting it multiple times with bottles or jumping on it.

- Winning: Defeat the final boss.
- Losing: Pepe's health bar reaches zero.

Coins restore health. Bottles break upon impact and are removed from the inventory. Use strategic moves to navigate through enemies and obstacles to win the game.
