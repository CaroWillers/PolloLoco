EL POLLO LOCO

Join Pepe on a wild desert adventure to defeat the giant chicken and save the day!

Key Legend for Pepe's moves

  • walk left: Left Arrow Key
    Pepe moves left.
  • walk right: Right Arrow Key
    Pepe moves right.
  • jump: spacebar
    Pepe jumps over obstacles or enemies.
  • throw bottle: Key D
    Pepe throws bottle.

The Story of Pepe

Pepe, the brave Mexican, embarks on an exciting adventure through the hot desert. Along his journey, he encounters dangerous enemies in the form of wild chickens blocking his way.

However, Pepe can defeat these chickens with throwing bottles he collects from the ground. Besides the chickens, there are also valuable coins to be found in the desert, which Pepe can collect to regain health.

At the end of his journey, a great final boss - a giant chicken - awaits to be defeated in order to win the game.
